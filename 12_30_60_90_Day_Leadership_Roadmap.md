# 12 - 30 / 60 / 90 Day Leadership Roadmap
## Assessment Question
### "What would you do in the short, medium and longer term?"

---

# Overview

The first ninety days are about creating confidence.

Confidence in leadership.

Confidence in capability.

Confidence in compliance.

Confidence in the future.

The objective is not to deliver as many training courses as possible.

The objective is to establish a sustainable capability framework that supports Australian Vintage for many years.

---

# Risks

Potential risks include:

Operational priorities.

Vintage demands.

Resource limitations.

Change fatigue.

Historical practices.

Competing systems.

Resistance to change.

These risks are reduced through:

Visible leadership.

Strong communication.

Evidence-based decisions.

Incremental implementation.

Stakeholder ownership.

---

# Leadership Reflection

The first ninety days are not about proving expertise.

They are about building trust.

By investing time understanding the business before changing it, I believe Australian Vintage gains something far more valuable than a new training program.

It gains a sustainable capability framework built on evidence, collaboration and operational understanding.

That foundation supports every future improvement the organisation chooses to make.
