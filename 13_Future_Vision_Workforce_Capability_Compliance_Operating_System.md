# 13 - Future Vision
## Building an Industry-Leading Workforce Capability & Compliance Operating System

---

# Vision Statement

My vision extends beyond delivering training.

I believe Australian Vintage has an opportunity to become one of Australia's leading examples of an integrated Workforce Capability & Compliance organisation.

The objective is not to purchase another software package.

The objective is to build an operating model where people, systems, knowledge and governance work together seamlessly.

The result is an organisation that is:

- Safer
- More capable
- More efficient
- More consistent
- More audit ready
- Better prepared for future growth

---

# The Opportunity

Australian Vintage already possesses significant strengths.

Highly skilled people.

Established production systems.

Laboratory capability.

Quality systems.

Food Safety systems.

Engineering capability.

Strong brands.

The opportunity is to connect these strengths through common governance.
