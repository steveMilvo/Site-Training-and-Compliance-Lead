# 05 - Workforce Capability Governance Framework
## Assessment Question
### "How would you improve operational performance across the business?"

---

# My Core Belief

Training alone does not improve operational performance.

Competent people improve operational performance.

Competency is achieved when people have:

- Clear expectations
- Access to current information
- Practical experience
- Appropriate coaching
- Formal assessment
- Ongoing reinforcement
- Leadership support

The objective therefore is not simply to manage training.

The objective is to build a Workforce Capability Governance Framework.

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term objective is to develop an organisation where capability is visible.

Every employee knows:

What is expected.

What competency they require.

Where to find current information.

How they will be assessed.

How they continue developing.

This creates a workforce where learning becomes part of everyday operations rather than a separate activity delivered by the training department.

The role of the Site Training & Compliance Lead therefore becomes one of enabling operational excellence through people, governance and capability rather than simply coordinating training courses.
