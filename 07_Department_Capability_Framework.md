# 07 - Department Capability Framework
## Assessment Question
### "How would you improve collaboration across departments while supporting safety, quality and operational performance?"

---

# Introduction

One observation I have made throughout my career is that organisations often become structured around departments rather than around operational capability.

Every department develops its own documentation.

Its own processes.

Its own training.

Its own terminology.

Its own records.

Over time this creates duplication, inconsistency and operational silos.

My objective would not be to remove departmental ownership.

It would be to connect departments through a common Workforce Capability Governance Framework.

Every department maintains ownership.

Every department follows the same governance model.

# Long-Term Vision

Departments continue owning their operational expertise.

However every department contributes to one integrated Workforce Capability Governance Framework.

This improves consistency while maintaining operational flexibility.

Employees moving between departments encounter familiar governance processes, common competency standards and consistent expectations.

The result is improved collaboration, improved learning and a stronger operational culture supporting Australian Vintage's long-term success.
