# My Leadership Philosophy

My leadership philosophy is strongly influenced by Ronald Heifetz's concept of moving between the Dance Floor and the Balcony.

Great leaders cannot remain permanently on either.

## The Dance Floor

Working alongside people.

Listening.

Observing.

Coaching.

Building trust.

Understanding operational realities.

Learning how work is actually performed.

## The Balcony

Stepping back.

Looking at the entire system.

Identifying patterns.

Understanding governance.

Recognising risks.

Developing strategy.

Successful organisations require leaders who continually move between both perspectives.

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term objective is to create an integrated workforce capability ecosystem where:

Every employee has a digital capability profile.

Every competency links directly to approved SOPs.

Every document has one owner.

Every revision triggers review and retraining where appropriate.

Every department has real-time capability visibility.

Every leader has access to meaningful dashboards.

Every audit can be supported through evidence rather than manual investigation.

My objective is not simply to improve training.
