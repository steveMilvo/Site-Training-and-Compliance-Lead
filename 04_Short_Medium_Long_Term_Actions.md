# 04 - Short, Medium and Long-Term Actions
## Assessment Question
### "What are your short, medium and longer-term actions?"

---

# Strategic Philosophy

One of the greatest mistakes organisations make is attempting to solve long-term problems with immediate tactical solutions.

My approach is based on progressive maturity.

Every improvement should build upon the previous one.

The objective is not rapid change.

The objective is sustainable change.

For this reason I divide implementation into four phases.

---

Capability becomes visible.

# Closing Statement

The scenario presented by Australian Vintage is not simply a training challenge.

It is an opportunity to strengthen workforce capability across the organisation.

By progressing through Stabilise, Align, Integrate and Optimise, Australian Vintage can move beyond managing training activities and develop an integrated workforce capability ecosystem that supports safety, quality, operational excellence and sustainable business performance.

This phased approach ensures immediate operational needs are addressed while creating the foundations for long-term organisational capability.
